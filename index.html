Weiwei Xu is a 3rd-year Ph.D. student in Computer Software and Theory at Peking University (PKU), advised by Prof. Minghui Zhou at Open Source Software Data Analytics Lab. Before that, he received his Bachelor's Degree in Software Engineering from Soochow University in 2022.

His research interests span software engineering, with a focus on open source software supply chain, open source compliance, and open source digital sociology. He is also actively exploring intelligent software engineering and evaluation of generative AI.
